# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Stoneford is a public, remix-friendly starter world. We want discussion to
stay generous, focused, and useful.

Please:

- assume good faith and discuss creative disagreements respectfully
- critique writing, structure, mechanics, and prompts without attacking people
- be considerate when discussing translations, influences, and inspirations
- keep bug reports and PR threads concrete and actionable

## Unacceptable Behavior

This includes, but is not limited to:

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- hate speech or degrading language
- sexualized or unwelcome conduct
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- repeated bad-faith disruption, trolling, or spam

## Scope

This applies to repository interactions such as issues, pull requests,
discussions, and other project-run community spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate or remove content, pause conversations, or limit
participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

For serious conduct problems, contact `info@worldlines.gg` with context
or screenshots.
